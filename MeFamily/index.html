    The 2025 Drift Masters line up is finally here

    The 2025 Driver Lineup

    First and foremost, 2025 will see many of our former champions returning to the grid, such as 2024 champion Lauri Heinonen, 2023 champion Conor Shanahan, 2017 champion Adam Zalewski and mutil-time champions Piotr Wiecek and James Deane. Alongside them on the grid will be, to name but a few of the many fan favourites returning, Duane McKeever, Kevin Piskolty, Benediktas Cirba, Tor Arne Kvia, Kuba Przygonski and the return of Japanese driver, Naoki Nakamura for a full season once again. We’ll also see a number of drivers joining the grid for their first full season in the championship, such as France’s Jason Banet, Finland’s Jarkko Jylhä, Espen Rohde from Norway and Cypriot driver, George Christoforou. The 2025 grid will then also see a very interesting addition as former Drift Masters commentator David Egan will jump out of the commentator seat and into the driver seat for the 2025 season, alongside his Irish teammate, youngster Conor Falvey, who made his mark last season by winning the title of 2024 rookie of the year. We’re sure you’ll agree that this lineup is befitting of the calibre of competition you’ve come to expect from Drift Masters, and will without a doubt guarantee some of the most explosive rivalries and breathtaking fights on track we’ve ever seen, that are bound to come down to the wire after another off-season of improvements and lessons learned.
