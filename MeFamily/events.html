Round 1 Italy

25 Apr 2025 - 26 Apr 2025

A new addition to the Drift Masters calendar in 2025, The Autodromo di Vallelunga Piero Taruffi is a racing circuit situated 32 km north of Rome, Italy, near Vallelunga of Campagnano. Vallelunga was built as a 1.77km sand oval in 1951.

Round 2 Spain

17 May 2025 - 18 May 2025

A new addition to the Drift Masters Calendar in 2025, The Circuito de Madrid Jarama - RACE, is a motorsport racetrack located in San Sebastián de los Reyes, 32 km north of Madrid. It was home to the Spanish Grand Prix nine times between 1968 and 1981, and the Spanish motorcycle Grand Prix 15 times between 1969 and 1988.

Round 3 Finland

6 Jun 2025 - 7 Jun 2025

Power Park in Finland, with its mesmerising backdrop and unparalleled fan energy, is ready to surpass last year’s phenomenal debut. The adrenaline-pumping rollercoasters and custom-built circuit make for a uniquely thrilling setting. With even more tickets available for our return to Finland, anticipate the roar of the Finnish fans to reach unmatched levels, creating an atmosphere that resonates with the intensity of the Northern Lights.

Round 4 Ireland

28 Jun 2025 - 29 Jun 2025

At Mondello Park, the heart of Irish drifting, we return to where legends are made. This circuit, woven into the fabric of Drift Masters, is set to host another chapter of unforgettable action with an electric atmosphere to boot. The Emerald isle’s most passionate fans will be out in full force, creating an unmissable spectacle that defines the spirit of Irish motorsport.

Round 5 Latvia

25 Jul 2025 - 26 Jul 2025

Next up on the calendar and much to the delight of the DMEC fanbase, the series will once again return to the firm fan-favourite Bikernieki Circuit in the Latvian capital of Riga, where thousands of delighted fans will descend to the Baltic country for one weekend only on the last weekend of July. This track is notorious for its high-speed entry and risk-reward layout, so expect surprises galore as the championship chase hots up as the second half of the season get underway. It’s Riga baby!

Round 6 Germany

14 Aug 2025 - 17 Aug 2025

If it’s unique venues you want to see, then Germany’s Ferropolis is one event you definitely need to witness at least once in your life. The aptly name “City of Iron” in the German town of Dessau, is an open-air colosseum set beneath the backdrop of huge abandoned 20th-century industrial machinery. For the penultimate round of the 2025 season and with no margin for error, the driver grid will be giving it their all under the night lights of this amazing venue to find out who will take one step closer to being crowned the 2025 Drift Masters champion.

Round 7 Poland

12 Sept 2025 - 14 Sept 2025

Our 2024 season’s Grand finale at Poland’s PGE Narodowy is set to be a historic showdown like none other. Building on last year’s record-breaking event, we’re aiming to fill all 55,000 seats across both days of the event, creating an amphitheatre of drift fans that will no doubt be heard the World over. The Polish capital of Warsaw, with its rich history and vibrant culture, provides the perfect backdrop for the crowning of our 2024 Drift Masters Champion. This event isn’t just a finale; it’s a celebration of the culmination of a decade of Drift Masters.
